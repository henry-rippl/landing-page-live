About Rippl

Rippl is a new way to remember.
Online and collaborative, rippl is a platform for exploring history through the lives and memories of the people that build it. The world is full of interesting people with interesting stories, and we don't want them to be forgotten.

Memories

Memories are the building blocks to a person's rippl. Some of these meories already exist and need only be gathered from social profiles, attics, and family albums, while other memoires have yet to be shared or have been overlooked.


People

Unlike current "profiles", a person's rippl page is built collaboratively by others. Each memory is placed into specific "eras" of a person's life that others may add to and explore. We all have unique stories to share but don't always have the chance to share them.


History

Connect the memories, and you have a person. Connect the people, and you have a history. Each rippl page is placed into communities to build a local legacy that will be explored, revisited, and added to for generations to come.
