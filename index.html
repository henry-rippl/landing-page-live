About Rippl

Rippl is a new way to remember.
Online and collaborative, Rippl is a platform for exploring history
through the lives and memories of the people that build it. The world
is full of interesting people with interesting stories, and we
don't want them to be forgotten.

The Team

Hello! This is Henry, Kevin, and Raja.
We are recent Stanford grads, living and working together in San
Francisco, dedicated to changing the way the internet remembers people.
Having experienced loss and the current inadequacies of dealing with it
online, we figured it was time to change it ourselves.
Together, our skills include user-centered design, experiential design and
research, UX/UI design, music, woodworking, photography, tinkering,
cooking, surfing, traveling, and much more.
We are excited to be bringing you Rippl, and we hope to be
sharing memories with you soon!
